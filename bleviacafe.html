About BLÉVIA


Blevia—a place that hugs the warmth of the breads and coffees, while holding the coziness of a cold served beverage. The soul of every now-and-then baker detached from its body, lingering somewhere in the surroundings of Blevia waiting for your visit. The very place that serves food crafted with care and quiet devotion.

There is a quiet comfort that settles in the moment you arrive, where time seems to slow down just enough for conversations to stretch and thoughts to rest. Every corner carries a gentle sense of joy, as if even the air has learned how to welcome people softly. It’s not just a place to eat or drink—it’s a small escape from everything loud and rushed, wrapped in warmth, aroma, and a feeling that quietly stays with you long after you leave

À mon avis, a must visit.
